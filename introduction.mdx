Welcome to Panora, the ultimate bridge between Roblox and Discord! Panora is a powerful bot designed to make it effortless to connect your Roblox community with your Discord server. Whether you're managing a large group or a small community, Panora provides the tools you need to streamline communication, manage member counts, and achieve your goals seamlessly.

---

## Setup

Panora simplifies the integration of Roblox and Discord, offering a range of features that enhance both platforms' user experience. Our bot is designed to automate many of the manual tasks involved in managing a Roblox group and a Discord server, making it easier for you to focus on building your community.
