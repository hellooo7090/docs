<Steps>

<Step title="Log in to Panora">  
Visit our [Dashboard](https://app.panora.cc/) and complete the login process.

<Frame>  
<img src="/images/Screenshot_80.png" alt="Panora login screen" />  
</Frame>  
</Step>

<Step title="Select the '+' Button">  
On the sidebar, click the `+` button to begin the workspace creation process.

<Frame>  
<img src="/images/Screenshot_83.png" alt="Sidebar plus button" />  
</Frame>  
</Step>

<Step title="Select a Guild">  
Choose one of your owned Discord servers from the prompt.

<Frame>  
<img src="/images/Screenshot_84.png" alt="Discord guild selection screen" />  
</Frame>  
</Step>

<Step title="Select a Roblox Group">  
Select one of your owned Roblox groups to link with your workspace.

<Frame>  
<img src="/images/Screenshot_85.png" alt="Roblox group selection screen" />  
</Frame>  
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm Selections">  
Review your selections and click **Confirm** to complete the process.

<Frame>  
<img src="/images/Screenshot_86.png" alt="Confirmation screen" />  
</Frame>  
</Step>

</Steps>
