---
title: Buying Elite  
description: "Panora makes upgrading to the Elite Plan quick and simple!"  
icon: "star"  
---

<Steps>

<Step title="Log in to Panora">  
Visit our [Dashboard](https://app.panora.cc/) and complete the login process.

<Frame>  
<img src="/images/Screenshot_67.png" alt="Panora login screen" />  
</Frame>  
</Step>

<Step title="Select a Workspace">  
Choose any of your workspaces from the Dashboard.

<Frame>  
<img src="/images/Screenshot_68.png" alt="Workspace selection screen" />  
</Frame>  
</Step>

<Step title="Click on Your Account Profile">  
In the bottom-left corner, click your profile, then select **Upgrade to Elite**.

<Frame>  
<img src="/images/Screenshot_69.png" alt="Account profile menu" />  
</Frame>  
</Step>

<Step title="Select the Elite Plan">  
On the blue Elite Plan card, click **Upgrade Now**.

<Frame>  
<img src="/images/Screenshot_70.png" alt="Elite plan selection" />  
</Frame>  
</Step>

<Step title="Begin Checkout">  
Provide your email and select your preferred payment type.

<Note>Additional fees may apply to the final price.</Note>

<Frame>  
<img src="/images/Screenshot_71.png" alt="Checkout form" />  
</Frame>  
</Step>

<Step title="Enter Payment Information">  
Input the required payment details as prompted.

<Warning>
  Paying with a credit card requires additional verification to prevent **credit
  card fraud**. You can avoid this step by choosing another payment method.
</Warning>

<Frame>  
<img src="/images/Screenshot_72.png" alt="Payment information screen" />  
</Frame>  
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm Purchase">  
Review your order summary carefully to ensure all details are correct. Click **Complete Purchase** to finalize your order.

<Frame>  
<img src="/images/Screenshot_73.png" alt="Order confirmation screen" />  
</Frame>  
</Step>

<Step title="Receive Confirmation and License Key">  
After completing your purchase, you'll receive a confirmation email containing your order details and license key.

<Frame>  
<img src="/images/Screenshot_74.png" alt="License key email" />  
</Frame>  
</Step>

<Step title="Copy License Key">  
Highlight and copy the license key from your confirmation email.

<Frame>  
<img src="/images/Screenshot_75.png" alt="Copying license key" />  
</Frame>  
</Step>

<Step title="Redeem the License">  
Visit our [Discord Server](https://discord.com/invite/2qsxZHfaTG) and use the `/redeem` slash command with your copied license key.

<Frame>  
<img src="/images/Screenshot_76.png" alt="Redeem license command in Discord" />  
</Frame>  
</Step>

<Step title="Elite Redeemed">  
Congratulations! Youâ€™ve successfully redeemed the Elite Plan. Explore the benefits of both Free and Paid plans on our [pricing page](https://panora.cc/pricing).

<Frame>  
<img src="/images/Screenshot_77.png" alt="Elite plan redeemed" />  
</Frame>  
</Step>

</Steps>
